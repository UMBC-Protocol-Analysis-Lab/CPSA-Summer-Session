To earn a certificate, at least 70% of the assignments must be attempted, and at least a 60% must be earned on each of the assignments for it to count.
Assignments that receive a satisfactory score of 60% will be given the full percentage, and assignments that do not receive a satisfactory percentage will be given zero percent.
For example, if the final exam is completed and a score of 80% is reached, then 25% will be given towards the 70% threshold required to earn the certificate.

Please do not attempt to game the system, we want you to complete most if not all of the assignments in the class.
You will learn more if you complete all of the homeworks, surveys, and do the final assignments and projects, and that will prepare you better to do cybersecurity and computer science research.
